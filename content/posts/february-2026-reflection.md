## Overview

The last 2 months have been good, if a little tiring at times. It's been really
busy with everything going on at work and all the socialising (mostly through
dating) but I do feel like I'm having a good time and getting out of my comfort
zone more which is an improvement.

January definitely set the tone for this and February has been about
consolidating some of that, but it was good to get a lot of the little goals
out early so I could build into some of the bigger ones.

## Professional Growth

People management has been going well from what I can tell. The tension I felt
towards the end of the year with Joe has eased as we've transition from peers
to a managerial relationship and I've tried to focus on making him feel
supported and empowered instead of subdued.

The team dynamic also seems to be going well. Victor is happy over in Dublin
and the London-based contingent make it into the office regularly for our team
day which is nice. Meetings have a relaxed tone to them and there's lots of
joking around, so my hope is that people enjoy what they're doing.

With the extra time that I have over Martin, I've been working on tackling each
team member's main pain point at work.

For Victor, this is about potentially moving him towards a remote contract and
allowing him to work from Brazil so he can spend more time with his daughter.
For Joe, this is mentioned above, but enabling him to work on more technical
projects and setting goals for him with the intention of getting him promoted
to a higher level.

For April, this is getting her more comfortable with the codebase we're working
in and allowing her to operate with more clarity and autonomy while owning
pieces of work. For Debbie, this is about instilling independence and autonomy
in her which frees up the rest of the team and makes her feel more adept and
technically competent.

There hasn't been any talk of Joe getting promoted yet, but we've set some
goals to allow us to have those conversations later in the year. April isn't
interested in this at the moment, so I'm comfortable allowing her to build on
her existing skills and settle in while life is more busy.

Martin and I have been having some development conversations recently, but I
need to find some concrete time in order to flesh these out for myself.

## Creative Output

Technically speaking, I have published 2 blog posts so far. One in January
internally at work around the release process, and one in February on here
around the around backwards compatibility.

This is good, but there are plenty of ideas I want to write about that I just
haven't had the time or confidence for. Some of them feel too decisive to be
writing about with my level of experience, but perhaps I need to find a way to
cross that boundary with smaller steps and get outside my comfort zone.

That being said, I was hoping to write more externally due to them requiring
less polish. I can write a short blog post for here in around half an hour and
have it reasonably well written, so perhaps I should try and up the cadence
there.

## Health & Fitness

### Training

Strength training has been going well, the busy-ness of Third Space at the
beginning and getting used to some of the machines definitely reduced the
intensity a little bit though.

I need to get back into the habit of tracking all my exercises now that I feel
I'm going to be here for a while, so that I can make sure I'm adding small
amounts each week. There are still some muscles (namely glutes) where I need to
figure out a strategy for progressive overloading given the machines available,
but that shouldn't stop me increasing the weights on the others.

I also ported opentracker.app to the monorepo as discussed originally, which
has allowed me to make some improvements there.

### Swimming

So... swimming has been a bit of a miss so far. The first swimming session
resulted in the loss of my hearing in my right ear for a day or so, the second
one resulted in many more ear problems and a lack of hearing due to trapped
water for almost 2 months that has only just resolved as I write this.

I am still keen to get back into it, but cannot justify it soon as it would be
good to retain my hearing. Perhaps there will come a time in a few months where
I work out how to use my earplugs properly, but for now it's on the backburner.

In its place I have started to pick up running a little more again which might
be fun, but we'll see if that sticks around or ends up being background work.

### General Care

Lots of progress here! I ended up getting an appointment with a dermatologist
down the road from us who prescribed me some steroid cream which has mostly
cleared up my psoriasis. I'm waiting to see how this goes over the next couple
of months and whether it returns, but for now it's nice to have made a change
in this area and see improvements.

I've also started on Invisalign through my dentist, which will include a course
of teeth whitening at some point during the treatment.

I don't necessarily have any other goals in this area at the moment as both
have been reasonable financial investments, but I'm sure I'll find some more to
explore in the coming months.

## Relationships & Social Life

### Platonic

This one has been a little more chaotic. We've hosted a party at our flat which
was nice, but it was quite the cross-over of friendship groups which made it a
little bit strange. Being the host, I also didn't get as much time as I'd have
liked to chat to everyone.

Rob and Leyla dating has also fragmented the group quite a bit as no one at
work knows about it aside from me, and it has introduced friction between him
and Lucie on top of that. It's been alright for me, I get to spend time with
two separate groups but there's also the difficulty of not being able to
discuss with anyone.

I have met some new people at gatherings, but nothing concrete yet. Perhaps I
need to focus on a person for a night and make it my aim to build more of a
friendship with them?

### Romantic

Okay so if any of the other sections are below par, it's because this one has
taken up a lot of my time. Looking at my calendar I must have been on almost 20
dates over the course of the two months, with lots of different people. While
this hasn't resulted in anything concrete as of yet, it's been a big learning
experience and allowed me to get out of my shell a bit more while taking risks.

I've started to understand again what aspects of a person matter to me and who
I connect well with, and I've had some new experiences which have been good.
It's improved my self confidence and made me feel more assured that the work I
did over the last 2 years was valuable.

## Financial Literacy & Growth

Consolidation of the childhood ISAs actually happened very early on, perhaps
triggered before the new year had even rolled in. Those have progressed and now
sit within my Starling account, but it would be good to move them out of there
into something earning more interest.

One goal for the next couple of days is probably to sort out my ISA allowance
off the back of the discussion at the end of the year. My Vanguard account
could do with topping up and rebalancing the funds, as currently my risk
appetite could be a bit bigger given my age.

There hasn't been any progress on the investment side of things unfortunately,
but that might come in the new financial year once I've worked out where all my
funds sit.

I've submitted an enquiry around financial advisors, as I think it would be
good to chat through my finances with a professional and understand how I can
best use them.

## Hobbies & Exploration

Hobbies and exploration is probably lowest on the list at the moment. I haven't
got any trips coming up and I haven't managed to find the time for driving
lessons, so perhaps that will need to take more of a focus later in the year.

I'd still like to go on trips, but as always I need to find people who want to
go on those trips. Perhaps there's an action item here around finding some
ideas I think others would like and proposing them, instead of waiting for the
other way around.

## Home & Environment

It's hard to remember some of the improvements I've made over the last 2
months. We adorned the flat with a lot of plants back in January which was a
great idea (although we are now trying to keep them alive), but I can't point
to another one unfortunately.

Perhaps this is something to make more of a concerted effort towards? My room
could do with a lot of work in terms of more photos and I've been saying I want
to get those done for a long time now so perhaps it's worth putting in the time
and money for it.

## Overall Verdict

Broadly speaking it's been a busy 2 months. As Laura would say, the term spikey
is probably applicable here. Some areas have had a lot of development and
others not so much, but provided that balances over the year or is serving me
as I would hope then it's alright.

I think the main thing to look into is the hobbies and exploration. I'll soon
find myself in the middle of May with everything being very expensive to book
and not actually take any leave, so I should start planning out something at
least even if that's for later in the year.
